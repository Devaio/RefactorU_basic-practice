What is a Bottle Opener?

They Open Bottles!

Many people may not have encountered a bottle opener before and are not sure what they look like.

According to Wikipeida: 

	A device that enables the removal of metal bottle caps from bottles.  
			More generally it might be thought to include corkscrews.

How can I tell them apart?

Bottle Opener
Generally have a hook or grip to pry off metal caps
Corkscrew
Long, swirled metal piece designed to pierce and pull corks out of bottles
